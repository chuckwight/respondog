About ResponDog


About us

ResponDog is an audience response application that poses your quiz questions or seeks opinions from your audience. Originally designed for teaching college classes, ResponDog is also great for presenters and trainers working with a group of business professionals. Keeping your audience active and engaged with the presentation is important. Polling helps, and ResponDog makes it easy.

ResponDog works with your learning management system (LMS) and returns participant scores back to the LMS grade book automatically. The instructor/presenter can also review the individual responses of each participant.

Learning Management System Integration

ResponDog is certified by 1EdTech to be conformant with the LTI Advantage Complete standard for learning tools interoperability. You can find the current 1EdTech certification document here. The certification ensures that you can configure compatible learning management systems with an LTI link to ResponDog. This allows your LMS to establish a trusted relationship with ResponDog without having to maintain any usernames and passwords. ResponDog reports the assignment scores back to the LMS grade book automatically. Your LMS administrator can register your LMS with ResponDog at our LTI registration page.

Privacy Policy

ResponDog protects its users' privacy to the maximum extent that allows the site to function as designed.
  • Personally identifiable information (PII) - Except for LMS administrators, instructors and facilitators who contact ResponDog directly (e.g., to establish an account or provide feedback), ResponDog does not store any user PII. ResponDog does not process, collect or store any user credit card or financial information whatsoever.
  • Digital Tracking - ResponDog does not use cookies, web analytics, web beacons, pixel tags or any other type of digital tracking devices. We do not store information about IP addresses, locations, or browser information of our users. We do not display any advertisements or enter into any service agreements with third party advertisers.
  • User IDs - During each user's session, ResponDog issues a secure token that is linked to an opaque user ID to ensure both privacy and security. For LTI users, this ID is provided by the learning management system (LMS). LTI is a secure messaging and communications standard developed by 1EdTech (formerly IMS Global Learning Solutions). LTI connects students and instructors with educational service providers. ResponDog is a certified LTI provider. Users who enter ResponDog through our home page are assigned a random ID number and are informed that they are participating as anonymous users.
  • Data and Information Collected - ResponDog collects and stores data and information about the quizzes, homework assignments and practice exams that are downloaded by our users, including customized assignments, individual responses to question items, assignment scores and timestamps. All data and information transmitted to or from ResponDog are protected by TLS/SSL encryption while in transit, and are stored in encrypted form on Google Cloud Datastore servers while at rest. All user data are solely owned by the user and may be deleted upon request to admin@chemvantage.org.
  • How We Use Data and Information that We Collect - For LTI users, individual user scores, data and information are available to the user, the instructor (as identified by the LMS) and to the LMS administrators. User scores may be reported by ResponDog directly back to the LMS grade book via the LTI Outcomes Service if this service is supported by the LMS.
    For all users, including anonymous users entering the site through our home page, we use scores, responses and user comments/feedback to improve the quality of individual question items in the site as well as site navigation and user experience. We also use timestamps to automatically delete user data and LMS accounts after one year of inactivity.
  • Information That We Share - We will access, use, preserve, and/or disclose user information if we reasonably believe it necessary to satisfy a valid and legally enforceable warrant, subpoena, court order, law or regulation, or other judicial or administrative order. Otherwise, ResponDog does not and will not share any user data or information with anyone except the user's LMS and with instructors and administrators who are identified by the LMS through the LTI interface.
  • Google reCAPTCHA - ResponDog uses the Google reCAPTCHA tool on its LTI registration page to detect bots and prevent spam and abuse. To register your LMS with ResponDog you must accept the Google reCAPTCHA Terms of Service and the Google API Terms of service. This is not required of users who are accessing ResponDog resources through the LTI interface (i.e., students and instructors) or users who enter ResponDog through the home page anonymously.
