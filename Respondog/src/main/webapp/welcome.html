Welcome to ResponDog!

Whether you are teaching a college class or presenting a training seminar to a
group of business professionals, you need your audience to be focused and attentive.

Active audience polling helps, and ResponDog makes it easy.

ResponDog is an LTI app that works with your learning management system to create
audience response polls for posing pop quiz questions or gauging students' opinions.
Polls are also useful for showing students how their answers compare to their classmates,
for taking class attendance and discouraging tardiness.

Scores on ResponDog polls are returned to your LMS grade book automatically, saving
you work, and ResponDog is free for non-commercial educational use.
